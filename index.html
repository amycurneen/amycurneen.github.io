A Data Scientist with a Passion for Visualizations

DATA SCIENTIST BY TRADE


Data science is about finding answers through raw data. But the story behind the data is just as important. This story is not only a way to help us solve the immediate issue at hand, but to look at the big picture as well and see what else can be gleaned from the data.

ENGINEER BY TRAINING


With a Master’s in Mechanical Engineering, I have worked in product development, primarily on electro-mechanical systems. Product development has given me the skills to build products or systems that offer a strategic value to the customers or others in the organization. 

DESIGNER BY PASSION


Over the years, my creative passion has evolved from illustration to web development to data visualization. The central theme to all of these outlets is communication: being able to communicate ideas through text and images in a creative and engaging manner. Communicating the data so it is understandable, relevant, and pertinent to clients.
